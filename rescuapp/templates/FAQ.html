FAQ
You have questions,we have answers.



Does it cost anything to get a resqU account?

Nope! Having a resqU account is completely free!

What products are available for purchase?

You can purchase anything that is currently available at the Princeton University Store. If your request is not in stock, you will be notified.

How much notice do I need to give for my delivery?

The requests for delivery must be delivered before midnight the night before. This will ensure that your order has enough time to be processed so that when you wake up your package is at your door.

Where will my purchases be dropped?

The purchases will be placed outside your door by the set delivery time. Once the package is dropped off, we are no longer liable for anything that happens to the purchases you have made.

What days does resqU offer delivery service?

We currently deliver on Friday and Sunday mornings, but are in the process of adding more available days to our service.

How does payment work?

Payment will be processed through Venmo. Your payment will not go through until it has been guaranteed that your requests are in stock, and your delivery will not be made until your payment goes through.

Can I change my order online?

You can delete your order before the payment goes through, and submit a new request with the change.
